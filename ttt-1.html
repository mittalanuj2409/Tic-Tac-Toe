Select Your Marker

My Marker

    Steps:

  • Select a marker as your symbol.
  • Toss a coin to decide who plays first.
  • Accept my challenge and try to defeat me.

    How to play:

  • A 3x3 arena is given. Click to select a cell at your turn.
  • A player wins if her marker is positioned in all cells of a row, a column or a diagonal.
  • Game ends if a player wins or all cells are filled resulting in a draw.
